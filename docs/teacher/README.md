# Teacher-facing research package

This folder contains materials that are suitable to share with the supervisor.

## Research focus (three core mechanisms)

The whole package is framed around **three core mechanisms** of a
relationship-aware multi-agent companion, which map onto RQ1–RQ3:

1. **关系编排 (relationship orchestration)** — who speaks, who stays silent, who
   follows up, who summarizes, and when to invite the elder or stop (RQ1).
2. **社会线索引入话题 (social cueing)** — two or three AI roles first exchange a
   couple of sentences around a photo / object / topic, then invite the elder to
   join, lowering the pressure to start talking (RQ2).
3. **多智能体角色交互 (multi-agent role interaction)** — how the relationship
   roles divide labour and coordinate (resonance, follow-up, summary, boundary
   protection) so the conversation avoids being noisy (吵), inauthentic (假), or
   overwhelming (乱) (RQ3).

**Long-term memory, trust, and control are supporting evaluation dimensions and
ethical boundaries — not a fourth parallel research question.**

Scope honesty: these are research-preparation / Study 1 design materials. No real
elderly interviews or experiments have been run yet, and the prototype is not a
finished automatic system. Do not present them as completed results.
